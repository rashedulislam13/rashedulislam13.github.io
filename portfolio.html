Latest Project

Dual Axis Sun Tracker

A dual-axis sun tracker project is an innovative and efficient system designed to follow the sun's movement accurately in both the horizontal and vertical directions. Its purpose is to optimize the positioning of solar panels or other solar devices, such as solar water heaters

PIR Sensor Motion Detector

A PIR (Passive Infrared) sensor motion detector is a smart and efficient device used to detect movement in its surroundings. It relies on detecting changes in infrared radiation emitted by objects within its field of view.

Smart Dust Clean

The smart dust clean project is an innovative and environmentally friendly approach to cleaning and maintaining various surfaces using advanced technology and minimal resources. It involves the deployment of tiny, autonomous, and self-cleaning nanobots

Android App for ICE Dept.

The Teacher and Student Profile App project is a digital platform designed to streamline communication and enhance interaction between teachers and students. It serves as a comprehensive tool that allows teachers and students to create and manage their profiles

Automatic Attendance Count

The Automatic Student Attendance Count project is a modern and efficient solution to automate the process of taking attendance in educational institutions.

Smart CA Marks Showing

The Smart CA Marks Showing project is a modern and efficient solution to automate the process of showing CA marks in educational institutions.
